About Me

Kabwasa is a 23-year-old, Watsonville native who is putting 831 on the map.

Sharing his name with his grandpa, who immigrated to the United States as a scholar representing the Congo after the country gained independence, Kabwasa sets out to represent his hometown and lead a creative renaissance in his own groovy and melodic way.
